DHYUTHI 1.0

  • Dhyuthi is the annual flagship event of IEEE SCT SB, that kicked off with its first edition in 2017. With participants from colleges all over Kerala, Dhyuthi offers a plethora of technical workshops, engaging talks, captivating music and also, a jovial trip to some of the well-known hang outs in Trivandrum.

  • The prime focus is to provide an arena where students from different colleges get to interact and expand their social circle along with getting involved in various technical communities. Dhyuthi gained much popularity among students shortly after its launch, and has now emerged to become one of the most anticipated events of the SB.

MOMENTS

IEEE SCTSB

  • The IEEE student branch started off in SCTCE in 2009 with a few dozen members. After almost a decade, IEEE SCT SB is currently one of the most dynamic student branches with 4 societies and more than 100+ active members.

  • The SB caters to enhancing the technical and managerial skills of its students, and also aims to act as a platform where they could learn the benefits of teamwork, coordination and synergy. Along with a team of dedicated volunteers, the SB organizes workshops, talks, seminars, outreach programs and keeps its members updated about the latest advancements in technology.

SCTCE

  • Sree Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering (SCTCE), Trivandrum was established by the Govt. of Kerala in the year 1995 in memoriam of the Great Maharaja of Travancore and is affiliated to the University of Kerala with AICTE approval.

  • Exposing the young minds to the world of technology, the institution instills in them plenty of confidence and fortitude to face new challenges and triumph in their chosen areas. A systematic and scientifically planned methodology, together with a team of experienced and dynamic faculty distinguish the SCTCE learning experience.

BLOCKCHAIN

Demystifying 'the Blockchain'

Blockchain is this year's buzzword - but can it outlive the hype? The open-source ledger behind bitcoin is touted as revolutionary for everything from banking to health, but the jury is still out. In this beginner friendly workshop we demystify the hype of Blockchain by understanding how those words - block and chain tie up together, and then dive head first deep into the most popular Blockchain platform - Ethereum and create cryptocurrency out of thin air! Workshop 4

ROBOT OPERATING SYSTEM

Code a Robot!

Robot Operating System is not an Operating system. Confused? Well, let's break it down. Developing a robot with a computer brain requires a bunch of software tools on the computer side. ROS is a framework that gathers all these tools and manages how you develop code for your robot. So, instead of reinventing the wheel every time, ROS can help you by gathering all these tools and managing how you develop code for your own robot! In this session, we aim to impart knowledge on how you can code your own robot from scratch!
